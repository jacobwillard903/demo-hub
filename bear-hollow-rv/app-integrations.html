Integrations

The tools Bear Hollow already runs on, working as one system. Nothing gets replaced.
What connected looks like: July 9, 8:42 PM.

The weather service issued a severe thunderstorm warning for the park's county. Within a minute, all 61 occupied sites got a text with the heads-up and the storm shelter location by the bathhouse. 14 guests replied, every reply was answered. Ken found out about the storm from the thank-you notes.
