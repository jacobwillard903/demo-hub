Why Metered Electric Matters

A monthly tenant pulls $85 to $140 of electric a month. Misread one meter, skip one month, and it quietly comes out of your pocket. One park found it had recovered only $17.5K of a $70K electric bill.

Here, every read lands on the invoice as its own line item, outliers get flagged for a re-read before they bill wrong, and nothing gets skipped. Zero unbilled meters since March.
