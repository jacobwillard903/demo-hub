What's inside

1
Route Gaps

Cancellation saves and backfills. Skip requests get answered with another day, not accepted. Open slots go to a ranked waitlist and refill in minutes instead of dying.

2
AI Quote Responder

Missed calls get a text back in seconds. Beds, baths, pets, frequency, then a real quote from your rate card and a walkthrough booked onto a route day. Home-service businesses miss 27 to 62% of inbound calls, and 86% of voicemail hitters just call the next company.

3
Churn Radar & Payment Chaser

Every client risk-scored from skips, complaints, and payment drag, with a suggested save before they quietly leave. Overdue invoices get chased politely on a ladder, so you never send the awkward text.

4
Crew Board

Today's routes per team with confirmations. When a cleaner calls out at 6:45 AM, the re-route is proposed, client texts are drafted, and you approve the whole morning in one tap.

5
Financials & Integrations

A QuickBooks-synced money view with a cleaning-company P&L and an AI bookkeeper, wired into what you already run: ZenMaid, Stripe, the phone line, Google Business Profile.

6
Ask

One question box over the whole business. Who's at risk, what's open Thursday, who owes money, what came in while you were cleaning.
