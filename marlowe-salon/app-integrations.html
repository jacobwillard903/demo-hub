Integrations

Nothing was replaced. The AI plugs into what the salon already runs.
Square AppointmentsConnected

The book of record. Every booking, reschedule, and rescue the AI makes is written straight into the calendar the salon already lives in.

Square POSConnected

Tickets, tips, and retail sales flow in as they happen. Powers the dashboard numbers and the shelf counts on the retail page.

QuickBooks OnlineConnected

The AI bookkeeper categorizes transactions, splits tips, matches payouts, and keeps the P&L current. Dana never logs in.

Twilio SMSConnected

Every confirmation, waitlist offer, rebook nudge, and missed-call text goes out on the salon's own number.

Instagram + Meta DMsConnected

The 10 PM "how much for balayage?" DM gets answered in seconds, priced right, and booked, while everyone's hands are in foils.

Google Business ProfileConnected

Review requests go out after happy visits. New reviews come in with drafted replies waiting for Dana's approval. 4.9 and climbing.

GmailConnected

Booking confirmations, the monthly P&L email to the accountant, and supplier invoices, read and filed automatically.

Google CalendarConnected

Dana's personal calendar syncs both ways, so a school pickup blocks her chair before anyone can book over it.

ShopifyAvailable

Put the take-home shelf online. Same inventory counts, so the site never sells a bottle the shelf does not have.

MailchimpAvailable

Seasonal promos and win-back campaigns to the client list, segmented by service history the AI already keeps.

SalonCentric orderingAvailable

Send the AI's draft backbar orders straight to the distributor instead of a phone call and a sticky note.

A "join the waitlist" button for the website and Instagram bio that feeds Cancellation Rescue directly.
